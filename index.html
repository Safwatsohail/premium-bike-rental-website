THE KNOWLEDGE GARAGE

Everything you need to know before you throttle up.

What documents do I need?
Valid driving license, passport, and credit card.
Is insurance included?
Basic insurance is on the house. But if you want full coverage for those "oops" moments, we've got premium packages. Ride hard, but ride covered.
Can I take the bike off-road?
Only if you rent our Adventure or Dirt bikes. Taking a Ninja H2 off-road is a crime against engineering. Don't do it.
What if I crash?
First, are you okay? That's priority #1. Second, call us immediately. Our 24/7 support team will handle the recovery.

RULES OF THE RIDE

The boring (but lethal) stuff.

  • Respect the Machine Treat the bike like it's your own. Any damage beyond normal wear and tear comes out of your pocket.
  • No Stunts (Unless on Track) Wheelies on Sheikh Zayed Road? Instant ban. Keep the rubber on the road unless you're renting for a track day.
  • Fuel Policy Full to Full. We give it to you with a full tank, bring it back full. Or we charge you premium + service fee.
